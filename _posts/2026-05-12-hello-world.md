Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I've been thinking about starting this blog for a while. As someone working at the intersection of AI, robotics, and autonomous driving, I find myself constantly swimming in ideas — about where technology is heading, about what it means for us as humans, and about the quiet moments of wonder that happen when you get a system to *finally* work.

## Why write?

There's something powerful about putting thoughts into words. Code is precise, but it doesn't capture the *why* — the intuition behind a design choice, the philosophical question lurking beneath a technical problem, or the sheer beauty of an elegant solution.

This blog is my space to explore all of that.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about three things:

**Technology** — Deep dives into AI, robotics, and autonomous systems. Not just the how, but the why and the what-if.

**Life reflections** — The human side of being in tech. Lessons learned, books that changed my thinking, and the art of staying curious.

**Ideas** — Half-formed thoughts, provocative questions, and the kind of thinking that happens when you stare out the window instead of at your screen.

## Let's connect

If any of this resonates with you, I'd love to hear from you. The best conversations happen at the edges — where different perspectives meet.

Here's to building, thinking, and creating — together.

*— Zhipeng*
